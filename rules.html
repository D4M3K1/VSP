Community Rules

  • ✅ Respect all members. Discrimination, harassment, or hate speech will not be tolerated.
  • 💬 Free speech is allowed, but religious arguments or attacks are not permitted to avoid unnecessary conflict.
  • ❌ No spamming, flooding, or unsolicited advertising.
  • 🔞 NSFW content must be clearly labeled, appropriately categorized, and never forced upon others.
  • 🕶️ This is an anonymous community — do not attempt to trace, reveal, or speculate about others' identities.
  • 🔐 Content shared within this community is private. Do not leak, repost, or share externally.
  • 🛠️ No malicious behavior — including attempts to exploit, hack, or overload any part of the platform.
  • 📢 Report any misconduct, abuse, or unwanted material through the contact form.
  • 👁️‍🗨️ Moderators have the right to remove content or users that violate the spirit of this community.

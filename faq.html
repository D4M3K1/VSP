Frequently Asked Questions

What is VSF?

VSF (Vines Special Forces) is a private, tactical-minded community built around anonymity, strategy, mutual support, and discussion. We aim to cultivate a space where members can exchange ideas, vent, express themselves freely, and find allies in various mental, social, and creative battles. It’s not just a group — it’s a support force.

Who can join?

Only those who are invited and accepted through the application form. This ensures the safety, integrity, and relevance of our member base.

Is my data or content safe here?

Yes. All posts and messages are end-to-end encrypted. Content is never shared outside this platform. We do not track or analyze personal information.

Are posts moderated?

Yes. We have light moderation to prevent abuse and maintain the community vibe, but we do not censor opinions unless they violate our core rules.

Can I remain anonymous?

Absolutely. You do not need to reveal your identity to be part of VSF. We encourage anonymity to promote raw, honest expression without fear of judgment.

What types of content are allowed?

You can post thoughts, articles, memes, images, or videos — as long as they respect the community rules. NSFW content must be properly labeled and kept in designated spaces.

How do I report a problem or user?

You can use our contact form to report any unwanted behavior, technical issues, or content violations. We respond quickly.

Is this a mental health or tactical support group?

In a way — yes. VSF is a hybrid community offering moral support, critical thinking, resilience building, and space for expression. Whether you’re here to share strategies or struggles, you're welcome.
